Never before has the demands for surfaces of the highest possible standard been higher for clubs and grounds managers. At SAS we believe in providing surfaces that promote good football the way it is meant to be played. This is achieved through acute understanding of turf, soil, drainage and playing quality.

Pitch Maintenance

Achieving optimal surface conditions for football is dependant on the maintenance practices carried out through the season. With increasing material and labour costs acute understanding of the value and benefit of maintenance activities is required to ensure standard are achieved in our existing climate. SAS has specialist knowledge based advisory services including ongoing seasonal support to ensure clubs invest it time and finance effectively.

Specialist renovations

Retaining winter playing quality is the greatest challenge for the majority of clubs, with pitches becoming increasingly muddy and uneven. Re-construction is not always an option to clubs but through accurate analysis of the key pitch limitations specialist renovation treatments are available. SAS in conjunction with industry partner has developed series of specialist works to tackle specific limitations.

Construction

Successful reconstruction projects encompass a series a detailed processes from project inception and investigations, design selection, contractor appointment, physical works through to completion of grow-in. It is only with all these facets successfully delivered that new pitches deliver clubs desired outcomes.

The construction of new pitches provide clubs with a rare opportunity to provide the club with a lasting legacy. Making informed decisions throughout this process is vital, SAS as an organisation develops and places teams together to ensure their pitches are successfully delivered from inception to completion.
